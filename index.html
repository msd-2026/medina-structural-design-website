About the Firm

Engineering support with practical judgment and clear communication.

Medina Structural Design provides structural engineering and permitting support services for residential and small commercial projects throughout Florida, New York, North Carolina, and Puerto Rico.

Led by a licensed Professional Engineer with more than 25 years of combined experience in structural, civil, transportation, drainage, and construction-related projects, the firm focuses on practical solutions, responsive communication, and technically sound designs tailored to each client's needs.

The firm supports homeowners, architects, contractors, and developers from engineering assessment and permitting support through construction documents and engineering coordination.

Structural + Civil Works

Coordinated engineering for design and permitting.

Structural engineering services include the design of framing systems, foundations, retaining structures, structural repairs, and rehabilitation projects.

Integrating structural and civil works helps streamline permitting, coordination, and compliance. Civil works include driveway access and roadway design, grading and site drainage design, and erosion control design commonly required during permitting and development.

Our Services

Structural & civil works engineering services for residential & commercial buildings projects.

Focused support for permitting, construction documents, site observations, and structural design needs.

Consultation / Reports

Our engineering assessments and written reports document observed conditions, provide clear recommendations, and outline next steps to guide homeowners, property managers, and realtors through structural and civil works challenges.

Structural Design & Construction Documents

Structural design and permit-ready construction documents for residential and small building projects, including framing, foundations, beams, columns, walls, details, notes, and supporting documents.

Construction Inspection

Construction inspection during project phases to support quality, coordination, and general conformance with the approved design intent throughout the work.

Civil Works

Civil works services provide site development and accessibility compliance support closely coordinated with structural design. Civil works include driveway access and roadway improvements, grading and site drainage, and erosion control.
